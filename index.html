Our project

Monarchs of Sylleria

A City of Wonder, Mystery, and Wealth

Sylleria is the vibrant, industrial heart of the world, sitting at the crossroads of every major trade route. While many have tried to conquer it by force, the city remains "free"—not because it lacks masters, but because its true power is hidden behind gold and secrets rather than stone walls.

The Fight for Power

Step into the "Great Game" as a Monarch from distant lands. You cannot rule Sylleria through decree alone; you must outmaneuver rivals in a never-ending ballet of intrigues. Every move you make is a calculated risk to claim the city's immense resources for your own kingdom.

Master the Guilds

In Sylleria, power waxes and wanes with your reputation. Build your influence by forming deep affinities with the powerful Guilds that run the city. As you level up your standing, you unlock unique alliances and industrial secrets—but beware, in this city, a "loyal" ally is only one bad trade away from becoming a rival.

The guilds of Sylleria

Mercenaries

Blades for hire. They do not have affiliations and their tactics are often crude, often relying on brute force alone to win the day. Their cards can be used by any monarch without limitations and will rarely showcase rule breaking mechanics.

Alchemists

Brewers of potions and adepts of transmutation. They provide services from common healing to supernatural mending of elements. Their strategy is often influenced by the alchemic duality making them versatile allies.

Clerics

The power of the church made manifest. They are makers of miracles and bearers of the sacred faith. They call upon the power of their god to protect or heal their allies and smite evil in all its forms.

Cultists

Zealots who made dangerous pacts with supernatural or unholy entities. They will call upon powerful abilities given to them by the abominations they worship. Wielding this kind of power can be as dangerous to them as to their enemies.

Druids

Guardians of the natural order. They command the forces of nature and the cycle of death and rebirth. They will call sturdy defenders and agile scouts to their aid while slowly building up their power.

Engineers

Master inventors but also more conventional masters of the craft. They design and build the most sturdy defences and the most advanced pieces of equipment. Their strategy relies on always having the right tool for the job.

Thieves

Rely on deception for survival. They disguise themselves as common merchants. Their tactics are often to hide their true intentions or themselves. Their card effects will often allow for unexpected moves or disable the opponent's ability to react.

Wizards

Wielders of arcane magic. Their abilities are quite unique if not always subtle. Their card's effect will be varied and difficult to reproduce as they stem from a mastery of magic.

Build your deck, create your strategy

Forge your perfect strategy by building your own custom deck. In Monarchs of Sylleria, you can combine cards from multiple guilds to craft a playstyle that's uniquely yours. Mix and match abilities, units, and interventions to outsmart your opponents on the battlefield.

Plan your moves and control the board

Play your cards to summon powerful allies, then command them across the board like chess pieces. Every tile, every move, and every position matters. Outmaneuver your opponent by controlling key spaces, setting traps, and launching coordinated attacks.
